Spam Fried Rice

All Recipes

Image credit: Krista on flikr

Ingrediants

  • 1 can of Spam, cubed
  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the cubed Spam and cook until browned, about 5 minutes.
  3. Push the Spam to one side of the skillet and add the remaining oil.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes until tender.
  5. Push everything to one side and pour the beaten eggs into the skillet, scrambling them until fully cooked.
  6. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing everything together. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot.
