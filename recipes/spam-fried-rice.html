Spam Fried Rice

Back to All Recipes

Image credit: Krista on flikr

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Spam, sliced
  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of kewpie mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Your favorite hot sauce (optional)

Directions

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced Spam and cook until browned on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  3. Put the Spam on a plate to cool. Add the remaining oil to the skillet.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes until tender (longer if veggies are frozen).
  5. Push everything to one side and crack the 2 eggs into the skillet, cooking however you prefer. I prefered fried, over medium.
  6. Put the cooked rice in a serving bowl. Add the vegetables and mix with soy sauce and kewpie mayo.
  7. Top the rice mixture with eggs and spam
  8. Add hot sauce to taste
