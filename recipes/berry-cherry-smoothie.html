Berry Cherry Smoothie

Back to All Recipes

Image credit: MariaPolna on pxhere

Ingredients

  • Handful of fresh or frozen spinach
  • 3/4 cup of frozen mixed berries
  • 1/4 cup of frozen cherries
  • 1/2 cup of plain greek yogurt (opt. nonfat)
  • 3/4 cup of pineapple juice

Optional

  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • 2 tablespoon of flax seed meal
  • 1 tablespoon of hemp seed hearts

Directions

  1. Add spinach to the bottom of your blender cup.
  2. Add berries, cherries, greek yogurt, and protein powder.
  3. Add any other optional additions.
  4. Pour pineapple juice over ingredients
  5. Blend until throughly mixed to your desired texture
  6. Pour into cup. Enjoy with a spoon or straw.
  7. Save extra for a second serving.
